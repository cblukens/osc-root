The DASH Repository: Harvard Scholarship, Global Impact

Spotlight: Access in Africa.

Reductions in library funding and currency fluctuation have forced African institutions of higher learning to cut back on subscriptions to scholarly journals. In 2007, the average cost for a one-year subscription to a physics journal was almost $2,200. By 2011, it was nearly $3,500. And prices keep rising.78% of academic libraries at African institutions have less than $100,000 per year to spend on journal subscriptions.

African Researchers Download from DASH

Scholars at African institutions often have limited access to subscription journals. Some have started turning to the DASH open-access repository, where thousands of journal articles by Harvard scholars are available for download, for free.

Since it launched in 2009, articles in Harvard's DASH repository have been downloaded over one million times and from nearly every country in the world. Approximately 2/3 of these downloads originated outside of the United States, and half of those (1/3 of all downloads) came from less-developed countries.
