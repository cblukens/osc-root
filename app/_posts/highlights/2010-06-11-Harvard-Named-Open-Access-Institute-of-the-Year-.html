--- layout: post title: "Harvard Named \"Open Access Institute of the Year\" " categories: about highlights permalink: /about/highlights/2010/06/harvard-named-open-access-institute-year/ ---

BioMed Central has named Harvard University one of its three open access institutes of the year.  The award recognizes institutions that have done the most to show leadership in taking steps to expand access to the published results of scholarly research.  Read the full announcement at BioMed Central.
