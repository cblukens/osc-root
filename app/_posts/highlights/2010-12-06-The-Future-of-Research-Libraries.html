"When I look back at the plight of American research libraries in 2010," says Robert Darnton, Director of the Harvard University Library, "I feel inclined to break into a jeremiad. In fact, I want to deliver three jeremiads, because research libraries are facing crises on three fronts; but instead of prophesying doom, I hope to arrive at a happy ending."
