In this white paper Simon Frankel and Shannon Nestor, attorneys at Covington & Burling, provide a thorough overview and careful analysis of legal issues related to public access policies. The paper credits Harvard and MIT with establishing a model open access policy that can be adopted by other institutions. Read the full paper in Science Commons.
